Lohith is a first-year PhD student in the DUCK Lab at Imperial College London, working on Neurosymbolic AI for financial markets. His research spans two areas: neurosymbolic reinforcement learning for portfolio construction, and deep generative models for simulating market dynamics under structural constraints. He is an incoming Quantitative Risk Intern at Capstone Investment Advisors and holds a first-class MEng from Trinity College, University of Cambridge.
